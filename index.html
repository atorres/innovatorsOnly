Innovators Only selects the best high schools from around the world to foster a research mindset in their students. Professors from Harvard, MIT and other leading US universities mentor these high school communities as students design their own projects and conduct hands-on research over a two-year period. Students whose work embodies the creativity and resolve at the core of the research mindset will be invited for summer research internships at leading universities in the USA.
